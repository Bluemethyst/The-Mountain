Create: Perfect World

This is a lightweight, Create focused modpack for Minecraft 1.20.1 put together by MrBeardstone and his mod team, and is the official pack used in his popular Create: Perfect World YouTube series. The pack is designed to create a Vanilla+ Minecraft experience, heavily focused around the Create mod and creating a fully immersive, active world.
